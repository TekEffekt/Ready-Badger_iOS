Bomb Threats

Most bomb threats are recieved by phone. Bomb threats are serious until proven otherwise. Act quickly, but remain clam and obtain information with the checklist from the download link below.

Who to Contact

  • Follow your local guidelines
  • Call Federal Protective Service (FPS) Police at 1-877-4-FPS-411 or 1-877-437-7411
  • Or call 911
